The Visionaries

Chief Executive Officer
Founder & Artistic Director

At the helm of our creative atelier, our Founder oversees the artistic evolution of the Onyx-Adire house. By reinterpreting ancestral Yoruba dyeing techniques through an avant-garde lens, they have transformed Adire into a medium for high-fidelity couture. Their vision is to marry the raw soul of the Nigerian dyer with the sophisticated silhouettes demanded by the global luxury market.

Managing Director
Chief of Operations & Brand Heritage

Orchestrating the house’s commitment to ethical luxury, our Managing Director curates the bridge between artisanal excellence and operational scale. From textile sourcing to boutique partnerships, they protect the brand's heritage integrity, ensuring that every collection remains a sustainable, high-end tribute to the artisans of Abeokuta and Lagos.
